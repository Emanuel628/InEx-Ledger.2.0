Common questions

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes — cancel from this page whenever you like. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds, no cancellation fees.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Nothing is deleted. Your records stay accessible on Basic. You can still read your full history and export data. You simply lose access to Pro-only features like receipt scanning, mileage tracking, and analytics.

Is my payment information secure?

InEx Ledger never sees your card details. Checkout is hosted entirely by Stripe — a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. Your credentials stay on Stripe’s servers at all times.

Is there a discount for paying yearly?

Yes. The yearly plan is equivalent to two months free compared to paying monthly. Select “Yearly” on the Pro card to see the adjusted price before checkout.

Can I manage more than one business?

Yes. Pro includes one business by default. Additional businesses are billed as an add-on. Use the stepper on the Pro card to set your count — it’s reflected in the price summary before you go to Stripe.

Can my accountant or CPA access the ledger?

Yes — Pro includes CPA access. Invite your accountant from Settings → CPA Access with read-only or limited write access. Access is fully revocable at any time.
