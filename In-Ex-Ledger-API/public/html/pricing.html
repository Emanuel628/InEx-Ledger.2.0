What the app actually does

The day-to-day financial operating system for a solo business

InEx Ledger is not trying to be everything for everyone. It focuses on the highest-value bookkeeping workflows that solo operators need to stay organized, accurate, and ready for tax time.

Income and expense tracking

Record transactions cleanly as money comes in and goes out. Keep your books current instead of backfilling a month of chaos at once.

Account tracking

Keep separate financial accounts organized so you can see where business money lives and what belongs to the business versus what does not.

Category management

Classify activity in a way that keeps your books more usable, more explainable, and more export-ready when you need to hand records off.

Receipt capture and storage

Keep receipts attached to the money they support instead of losing them to email, camera rolls, random folders, or “I know it’s somewhere.”

Mileage logging

Track business mileage in the same workspace as your financial records so your deductible activity is not split across five different apps and notes.

Exports for review and tax season

Generate cleaner outputs when you need to review performance, prep for taxes, or get records into a shape that is easier to share with an accountant.

Harder separation between business and personal

The app is built around keeping business finances contained in a dedicated workspace, which is one of the biggest quality-of-life upgrades a solo operator can make.

Support for more than one business

When you grow into multiple ventures, you can add additional business space instead of jamming everything into one messy ledger and hoping you untangle it later.
