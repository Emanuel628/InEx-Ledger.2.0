What the app actually does

The workflow solo operators actually need

InEx Ledger is not trying to be everything for everyone. It focuses on the bookkeeping jobs that make the difference: imports, receipts, invoices, review, and exports.

Transaction capture and cleanup

Bring income and expenses into the ledger quickly, then clean up what still needs category, proof, or judgment before it becomes a bigger problem.

Receipts and support files

Keep receipts, notes, and supporting files attached to the record they explain instead of losing the support trail across email, folders, and camera rolls.

Smarter categorization and review

Use category suggestions, review flags, and bookkeeping structure that make the ledger easier to trust and faster to hand off later.

Invoice records that stay connected

Send invoices from the same system you use for your books and keep the record, follow-up, and replies closer to the financial history they affect.

Mileage and vehicle support

Track mileage and vehicle-related support in the same bookkeeping system as the rest of the books so the evidence does not get split across separate tools.

CPA hand-off exports

Generate PDF and CSV workpapers that show cleaner totals, support coverage, warnings, and unresolved items before records leave the app.

Business-first structure

Keep business records in a dedicated ledger with accounts, categories, receipts, and exports that make the books easier to maintain and easier to explain.

Room for more than one business

When you operate more than one venture, you can add business space instead of forcing everything into one ledger and untangling it later.
