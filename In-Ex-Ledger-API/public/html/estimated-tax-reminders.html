Installment reminders

Installment reminders for solo operators who would rather not be surprised.

Estimated tax deadlines are rarely confusing because the dates are unknown. They are confusing because the business owner is busy, the estimate is sitting somewhere else, and the deadline only becomes real when it is already too close.

InEx Ledger surfaces reminders with the due date, countdown, a draft estimate when available, and a link to pay through the right authority.
