Why books get messy

Most problems come from disconnected records, not missing effort.

The expense is in one place. The receipt is in another. The mileage note is somewhere else. The invoice reply is buried in email. Tax time becomes reconstruction work.

What usually breaks

  • Imported rows still need real categories.
  • Receipts are missing or disconnected from the transaction.
  • Mileage and allocation support get patched in late.
  • Invoice replies live outside the bookkeeping record.

What InEx fixes

  • One place to track what still needs cleanup.
  • A support trail that stays with the record.
  • Warnings that explain what blocks export.
  • A stronger handoff when it is time to send the package out.
